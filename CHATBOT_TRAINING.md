## User Account Management

### Creating an Account (Sign Up)

**How to Sign Up:**
1. Click on the "Sign Up" link in the navigation menu
2. Fill in the required information:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email Address
   - Password
3. Verify your email address
4. Click "Create Account"
5. You will be redirected to the login page

**Account Requirements:**
- Must provide a valid email address
- Password must be secure and memorable
- All fields are mandatory

### Logging In

**How to Log In:**
1. Click on the "Login" link in the navigation menu
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Login"
5. You will be logged into your account

**Forgot Password:**
- Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page
- Enter your registered email address
- You will receive password reset instructions via email
- Follow the link to create a new password

### Managing Your Profile

**Accessing Your Account:**
1. Click on your profile icon or "My Account" in the menu
2. You will see your account dashboard with various options

**Editing Profile Information:**
1. Go to "Edit Your Profile"
2. Update your information:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email Address
   - Address
3. Click "Save Changes"
4. Confirmation message: "Profile updated successfully"

**Profile Sections:**
- **Account Settings**: Edit personal information
- **Address Book**: Manage delivery addresses
- **My Orders**: View purchase history
- **My Cancellations**: View cancelled orders
- **Wishlist**: View saved items

## Cart & Wishlist

### Shopping Cart

**Adding Items to Cart:**
1. Browse and select a product
2. Choose preferred size and color
3. Select quantity
4. Click "Add to Cart"
5. Product appears in your cart (cart icon shows item count)
6. Confirmation message: "Item added to cart"

**Managing Cart Items:**
1. Click the shopping cart icon
2. View all items in your cart with:
   - Product name and image
   - Selected size and color
   - Quantity
   - Unit price
   - Total price per item
3. Options available:
   - **Increase/Decrease Quantity**: Use +/- buttons
   - **Remove Item**: Click remove or trash icon
   - **Update Cart**: Changes are saved automatically

**Cart Summary:**
- Subtotal: Sum of all item prices × quantities
- Estimated Shipping: Calculated at checkout
- Total: Final amount to pay

### Wishlist

**Adding to Wishlist:**
1. Click the heart icon on any product
2. If not logged in: Prompted to login first
3. Confirmation message: "Added to wishlist ❤️"
4. Heart icon changes color to indicate it's saved

**Viewing Wishlist:**
1. Go to "My Account" → "Wishlist"
2. View all saved items with:
   - Product images
   - Prices and discounts
   - Availability status
3. Options available:
   - **Add to Cart**: Move item from wishlist to shopping cart
   - **Remove from Wishlist**: Delete item (Confirmation: "Removed from wishlist")
   - **View Details**: See complete product information

**Wishlist Features:**
- Login required to use wishlist
- Save items for later purchase
- Compare prices before buying
- Get notifications on price drops (if subscribed)

## Checkout Process

### Proceeding to Checkout

**Steps:**
1. Click shopping cart icon
2. Review all items and quantities
3. Click "Proceed to Checkout" or "Continue to Checkout"
4. You will be directed to the checkout page

**Login Requirement:**
- Must be logged in to proceed
- If not logged in, you will be prompted to login or create an account

### Billing Details

**Required Information:**
1. **Full Name** (mandatory): Your complete name
2. **Phone Number** (mandatory): Valid contact number for delivery
3. **Address** (mandatory): Complete delivery address
4. **Address Type**: Select from:
   - Home
   - Office
   - Other

**Using Saved Address:**
1. If you have saved addresses, they appear as options
2. Select "Default Address" to use your primary address
3. Or select from "Saved Addresses"
4. Edit address if needed

**Adding New Address:**
1. Fill in all required fields
2. Select address type
3. Choose if this is your default address
4. Click "Save Address"

### Order Review

**Before Placing Order:**
1. Review all items in your order
2. Verify quantities are correct
3. Confirm billing address
4. Check subtotal and total amount
5. Review any applicable discounts

**Order Summary Shows:**
- Item descriptions and quantities
- Individual item prices
- Subtotal amount
- Shipping costs
- Any applicable taxes or fees
- **Grand Total**: Final amount to be charged

## Orders & Purchases

### Placing an Order

**Final Steps:**
1. Verify all billing information is correct
2. Check order summary one final time
3. Click "Place Order" or "Confirm Order"
4. Upon success, you will see: Order confirmation page
5. Order ID is generated and displayed

**After Placing Order:**
1. Confirmation email sent to your registered email
2. Order ID provided for reference
3. Estimated delivery date provided
4. Payment confirmation included

### Viewing Order History

**Accessing Orders:**
1. Go to "My Account" → "My Orders"
2. View all your purchases with:
   - Order ID
   - Order date
   - Delivery address
   - Total amount
   - Current order status

**Order Statuses:**
- **Placed**: Order successfully created
- **Processing**: Order being prepared
- **Shipped**: Order sent to delivery
- **Delivered**: Order successfully delivered
- **Cancelled**: Order cancelled

### Order Details

**View Complete Order Information:**
1. Click on an order ID in "My Orders"
2. See detailed information:
   - Order ID and date
   - List of all items with:
     - Product name and image
     - Selected size and color
     - Quantity ordered
     - Price per item
     - Total item price
   - Billing address used
   - Contact number used
   - Subtotal amount
   - Shipping cost
   - Total amount paid
   - Order status and timeline
   - Estimated delivery date

### Order Cancellation

**Cancelling an Order:**
1. Go to "My Account" → "My Cancellations" (if available)
2. Or access order and look for "Cancel Order" option
3. Select reason for cancellation
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Order status changes to "Cancelled"

**Cancellation Policy:**
- Orders can only be cancelled before they are shipped
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
- You will receive cancellation confirmation via email

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Login

**Q: How do I create an account?**
A: Click "Sign Up" in the navigation menu, fill in your details (first name, last name, email, password), verify your email, and complete registration.

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the reset instructions sent to your email.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Yes, go to "My Account" → "Edit Profile" and update your email address. You may need to verify the new email.

**Q: Is my personal information secure?**
A: Yes, we use encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and payment information.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: We recommend using one account per person. Multiple accounts may lead to confusion with order tracking and wishlist management.

### Products & Shopping

**Q: Do you offer products for all ages?**
A: Yes, we have products for women, men, kids, and unisex categories including electronics and beauty products.

**Q: Are products in flash sale always available?**
A: Flash sale items are limited and available on a first-come, first-served basis. They may sell out quickly.

**Q: How are products rated?**
A: Products are rated on a 1-5 star scale based on customer reviews and feedback.

**Q: Can I see product details before adding to cart?**
A: Yes, click on any product to view full details including description, images, available sizes, colors, and customer ratings.

**Q: What sizes are available?**
A: Most clothing items come in sizes: XS, S, M, L, XL. Formal wear may include XXL.

**Q: What colors are available for products?**
A: Color options vary by product. Check individual product pages to see available colors.

### Cart & Wishlist

**Q: How do I add items to my cart?**
A: Select a product, choose size and color, enter quantity, and click "Add to Cart".

**Q: Can I save items to purchase later?**
A: Yes, use the wishlist feature. Click the heart icon on any product to save it. You must be logged in.

**Q: Can I change quantities in my cart?**
A: Yes, use the +/- buttons next to each item to adjust quantity. Changes are saved automatically.

**Q: Can I remove items from my cart?**
A: Yes, click the remove or trash icon next to the item you want to delete.

**Q: Is there a cart limit?**
A: No maximum cart limit, but you can only purchase items that are in stock.

### Checkout & Orders

**Q: Do I need to be logged in to checkout?**
A: Yes, you must be logged in to proceed with checkout and place orders.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept credit/debit cards, digital wallets, bank transfers, and cash on delivery (if available).

**Q: How long does delivery take?**
A: Delivery timelines vary. You will see estimated delivery date during checkout and after order placement.

**Q: Can I change my delivery address?**
A: You can change the address during checkout. Contact support immediately if you need to change after placing the order.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**
A: Yes, orders can be cancelled before they are shipped. Go to "My Cancellations" or contact support.

**Q: What is your return policy?**
A: Please contact our customer support team at customer@exclusive.com or +8801611112222 for detailed return policy information.

**Q: How do I track my order?**
A: Go to "My Account" → "My Orders" and click on your order to view status and tracking information.

### Discounts & Offers

**Q: How often do you have sales?**
A: We have regular flash sales, best seller promotions, and seasonal offers. Check the home page for current deals.

**Q: Are there discount codes?**
A: Follow our website for current promotions and discount offers.

**Q: Do you offer loyalty programs?**
A: Check with our support team for information about any loyalty programs.

**Q: Can discounts be combined?**
A: Typically one discount per order. Check terms and conditions for specific promotions.

### Shipping & Delivery

**Q: How much is shipping?**
A: Shipping costs are calculated based on location and are shown during checkout.

**Q: Do you offer free shipping?**
A: Free shipping offers may be available on selected promotions. Check the checkout page for details.

**Q: Can I track my shipment?**
A: Yes, after your order ships, you'll receive tracking information to monitor delivery progress.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Please contact support for information about international shipping options.

**Q: What if my order doesn't arrive?**
A: Contact our support team immediately with your order ID and they will investigate.

### Technical Issues

**Q: Why can't I add items to my cart?**
A: This may happen if you're not logged in, the item is out of stock, or there's a technical issue. Try logging in or refresh the page.

**Q: The website is loading slowly. What should I do?**

**Q: I'm having payment issues. What should I do?**
A: Check your payment method details, ensure your billing address is correct, and contact support if the issue persists.

**Q: How do I report a website bug?**
A: Contact our technical support team at support@exclusive.com with details of the issue.

### General Questions

**Q: How can I provide feedback?**

**Q: How do I unsubscribe from emails?**

**Q: Is the website mobile-friendly?**

## Important Notes for Chatbot Training

### User Intents the Chatbot Should Handle:

1. **Product Discovery**: Help users find products by category, search, or browsing
2. **Account Help**: Assist with login, signup, and profile management
3. **Order Support**: Provide order status, tracking, and cancellation information
4. **Shopping Assistance**: Guide users through cart management and wishlist
5. **Checkout Support**: Help with billing information and payment
6. **Technical Support**: Troubleshoot website issues
7. **General FAQs**: Answer common questions
8. **Contact Information**: Provide support contact details

### Context Variables the Chatbot May Need:

- User login status (logged in / logged out)
- Current page/section user is on
- Items in user's cart
- User's previous orders
- User's wishlist items
- User's saved addresses

### Response Guidelines:

- Always be friendly and helpful
- Provide clear, step-by-step instructions
- Use confirmation messages as shown
- Provide contact information for complex issues
- Confirm order details before placement
- Respect user privacy and security
